Our Story

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our business approach

Our Mission

To consolidate excellence across diverse industries by leveraging synergies between our subsidiaries, creating sustainable value for stakeholders, and making positive contributions to the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be Ghana's leading diversified conglomerate, recognized for innovation, excellence, and social responsibility across all our business sectors, while expanding our footprint across Africa and beyond.

Our Approach

We believe in strategic diversification, ethical business practices, and empowering local talent. Our approach combines global best practices with deep local knowledge to create sustainable businesses that deliver value.

Our Core Values

The foundation of everything we do

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all our endeavors, continuously improving and innovating to exceed expectations.

Integrity

We conduct business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices, building trust with all our stakeholders.

Innovation

We embrace change and continuously seek new ways to create value, solve problems, and improve our services.

Social Responsibility

We are committed to making a positive impact on society through our business practices and community initiatives.

Our Journey

Milestones in the growth of YAC Group Holdings

2018

Foundation

YAC Group Holdings was established with a vision to consolidate diverse business interests under one umbrella, starting with Seventeen17 Pharmacy.

2019

Expansion into Sports

Launched Club Consult Africa and Strike Ghana, expanding our portfolio into sports management and apparel manufacturing.

2020

Diversification

Established YAC Investments and YAC Media Productions, entering the investment and media sectors despite global challenges.

2021

Social Impact

Founded the Chibsah Foundation and Chibsah Farms, demonstrating our commitment to social responsibility and sustainable agriculture.

2022

Strategic Growth

Added Konacare Limited, RC Events, and Waterroam West Africa to our portfolio, expanding our reach in healthcare, events, and water purification.

2023

Current Expansion

Launched Bricks and Marbles Ghana Ltd and YussFathia Ventures, entering real estate development and general merchandising to complete our diversified portfolio.

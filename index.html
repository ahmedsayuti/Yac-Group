About YAC Group

Consolidating excellence across diverse industries

YAC Group Holdings is a dynamic consolidation of subsidiary companies operating across various sectors including healthcare, sports management, media production, agriculture, real estate, and more.

Founded on principles of excellence, innovation, and social responsibility, our group leverages synergies between our subsidiaries to create value for our stakeholders and contribute positively to the communities we serve.
